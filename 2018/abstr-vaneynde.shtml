Van Eynde, Frank: Adnominal participles

Adnominal participles show a mixture of verbal and adjectival properties. This paper presents an analysis that captures this hybrid nature. Section 1 provides a description of the data. It is larded with examples and quantitative info from Lassy Small, a treebank of contemporary written Dutch. Section 2 provides an analysis. It is cast in the notation of Ginzburg and Sag (2000).
