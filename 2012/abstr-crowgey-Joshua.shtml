Crowgey, Joshua: What's a Morpheme? The Zero Dravidian Negative and Typological Universals

In this poster, I draw attention to a putative typological universal, the morphemic realization of negation and a potential counterexample found in Old Kannada. I explore the relationship by casting the problem within the ontology provided by an implementation of the construction in a grammar from the LinGO Grammar Matrix.
