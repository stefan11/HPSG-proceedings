Creissels, Denis & Godard, Danièle: The Tswana Infinitive as a Mixed Category

After studying the morphological and syntactic properties of Tswana infinitives in some detail, we argue that a mixed category approach is more adequate than a phrasal approach to account for the combination of their common properties with the two different uses they are found in.
