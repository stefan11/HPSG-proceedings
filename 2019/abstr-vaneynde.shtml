Van Eynde, Frank: Nominalized infinitives in Dutch

Nominalized infinitives have the external distribution of an NP, but their internal structure is a mixture of verbal and nominal characteristics. The purpose of this paper is (1) to provide a description of the Dutch nominalized infinitive, (2) to model it in terms of HPSG and (3) to compare the result with Rob Malouf’s mixed category treatment of the English gerund.
